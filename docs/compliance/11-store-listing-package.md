## Compliance guardrails for ALL marketing
- ✅ Use: "preserve", "organise", "support", "companion", "remember", "memory journey".
- ❌ Avoid: "diagnose", "treat", "therapy", "clinical", "medical", "assessment", "slows
  dementia", "improves memory", or any claim of health benefit/outcome.
- "Dementia support" / "memory-loss support" must always read as **emotional/
  organisational** support, never treatment or clinical benefit.
