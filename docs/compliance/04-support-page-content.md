## B. FAQ

**What is RemyNest?**
RemyNest is an AI-powered memory preservation platform. It lets you store, organise,
search and share memories, notes, photos and videos, with helpful reminders and
AI-assisted organisation and summaries.

**Is RemyNest a medical or healthcare app?**
No. RemyNest is a personal memory-preservation and organisation tool. It is **not** a
medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or any clinical
or cognitive assessment. It can be a supportive companion for individuals and families,
including those affected by memory loss, but it makes no medical claims and should not be
used for medical decisions.

**How does the AI work, and what does it do?**
RemyNest uses AI to categorise and organise your memories, generate summaries, and
improve search. AI output is for convenience only and may contain errors — please review
it and never rely on it for medical, legal or financial decisions.

**Does RemyNest use my content to train AI models?**
No. We do not use your content to train third-party AI models, and our AI provider is
contractually prohibited from training its models on your content.

**Who can see my memories?**
Only you and the people you explicitly invite or grant access to (such as family members
or caregivers). You control sharing and permissions. Your data is isolated from other
accounts and protected by access controls and encryption.

**How do I export my data?**
In the app: **Profile → Settings → Privacy → Export my data**. You'll receive your data
in a structured, machine-readable format.

**How do I delete my account?**
In the app: **Profile → Settings → Privacy → Delete my account**. Your account is
deactivated immediately and permanently deleted within 30 days (sooner in practice),
including from backups within 30 days. You can also email privacy@remynest.com.

**How is my data protected?**
We use encryption in transit and at rest, database-level access isolation
(Row-Level Security), least-privilege access, secure cloud storage, and continuous
monitoring. See our Privacy Policy for details.

**Where is my data stored and processed?**
Your data is processed by our cloud providers and may be processed outside your country,
including in the United States, under appropriate safeguards (such as Standard
Contractual Clauses). See the Privacy Policy.

**How do subscriptions and billing work?**
Some features require a subscription. If you subscribe through the App Store or Google
Play, manage or cancel it in your store account settings. Cancelling stops future
renewals; access continues until the end of your paid period.

**How do I turn notifications on or off?**
Manage notifications in the app under settings, or in your device's notification
settings at any time.

**I'm a caregiver / family member — how does shared access work?**
Account holders can invite family members or caregivers and configure what they can
access. Please only use shared access as authorised by the account holder.

**Who do I contact about privacy or a data request?**
privacy@remynest.com (or dpo@remynest.com). You also have the right to complain to your
data protection authority.
